## How to Use the App
No login is needed. Just open the link above on a laptop or phone and you'll see a map of Bangalore with all localities marked. Here's how to use it in a live call or out in the field:
1.	Open the map. It loads centred on Bangalore, showing every locality as a marker.
2.	Click any locality to select it. A panel opens (a bottom sheet on mobile) listing every apartment complex in that locality.
3.	Look for the orange ring. Any locality within about 3.5 km of the one you picked lights up automatically, so you can judge nearby options at a glance.
4.	Search an apartment by name using the search bar at the top. The map flies straight to it and shows its locality and pincode.
5.	Turn on transit layers if you need them. Four toggles (Metro, Bus, Railway, Auto/Cab) are off by default; switch on only what you need to keep the map clean.
6.	Zoom in to see pincodes and, further in, individual apartment pins. Zoomed out, the map stays uncluttered.
7.	Hit Reset any time to clear your selection and toggles and go back to the default view.
